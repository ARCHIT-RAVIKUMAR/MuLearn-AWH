Who We Are

A community built on growth & collaboration

µLearn AWH is the official campus chapter of µLearn at AWH Engineering College, Calicut — part of Kerala's largest student skilling ecosystem with 64,000+ members and 400+ partner companies.

We break the echo chamber of traditional learning. Through Interest Groups, and a karma-based proof-of-work system, we help students build real skills, access exclusive internships via Launchpad, and grow together — week by week.

🎯

Proof of Work

Karma points are earned by completing real tasks — not just attending classes. Your profile is your portfolio.

🤝

Peer Learning

Engage in weekly peer sessions, monthly skill challenges, and quarterly innovation sprints.

⚡

Level Up System

Progress through 4 levels — from onboarding to unlocking Interest Groups in AI, Web, Design, and more.

🚀

Launchpad

Access exclusive internships and the Top 100 Series — opportunities only available to active karma earners.
